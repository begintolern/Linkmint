Title:
How Our Referral Program Works

Introduction:
Welcome to our referral program! Here’s the lowdown on how you can invite friends, earn bonuses, and get rewarded.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Invite Your Friends
Share your unique referral link with friends. You can find this link in your dashboard under the "Referrals" section.

Friends Sign Up and Engage
When your friends use your link to sign up, they’ll automatically be tagged as your referrals. As they start using the platform, you’ll both benefit.

Earn a 5% Bonus for 90 Days
For each new friend you refer, you’ll earn a 5% commission bonus on their earnings for the first 90 days. In other words, if they earn, you get a slice of that success as a thank-you.

Track Your Progress
You can see your referrals, their activity, and your bonus status right in your dashboard. Everything’s transparent and easy to follow.

Why It’s Worth It:
Our referral program is designed to reward you for spreading the word. It’s a win-win: your friends join a platform you trust, and you get a little bonus as a token of our appreciation.
